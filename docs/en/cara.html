Workshop – Don Quichotte 2080

La Grande Tourrache
Software : Krita

As part of a workshop with Lycée La Tourrache and David "Vyle" Levy, I reimagined Don Quixote in a futuristic setting.

In a post-apocalyptic world, Sancho Panza is reborn as a makeshift robot, assembled from salvaged scraps, including an abandoned trash can. A loyal companion to his master, he resourcefully carries his treasures through the remnants of a ruined future.The goal of this project was to explore character design and the world of concept art, reinventing iconic figures through a dystopian and technological lens.
