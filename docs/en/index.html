FAQ

What types of projects do you prefer working on?

I mainly work on motion design, 3D, and visual creation projects. I especially enjoy projects with a strong art direction or an animated identity.

How do you handle tight deadlines?

I plan every step from the start. This allows me to anticipate, stay efficient, and deliver on time, even on urgent projects.

Are you comfortable working with a creative director or in a team?

Yes, completely. I easily adapt to existing workflows and can also suggest visual directions when needed.

What type of studio or company are you hoping to join?

I'm open to any type of structure: creative studios, startups, companies, or independent teams. What matters to me is working with people who value creativity and visual quality.

How do you stay inspired?

I stay constantly curious — following design, film, video games, 3D, and tech, and running my own experiments. This helps me bring fresh, relevant ideas to every project.

Can you adapt your style to match a brand or project?

Yes. I adapt to each brand's world while bringing my own creative touch. Minimalist, futuristic, illustrative, or photorealistic, I can adjust my style to fit the brief.
