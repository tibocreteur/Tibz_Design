    Nemo

    E!ght
    Software : ADOBE Photoshop

    For E!ght's album Nemo, I designed the back cover of the vinyl, drawing inspiration from the original artwork created by the artist, as well as his character and the central theme of the album. Using his publications and visual universe as references, I aimed to visually convey the essence of his project.

    I also incorporated the idea of an interactive sleeve that functions like a mirror: to reveal the tracklist for sides A and B, the sleeve must be rotated. This layout symbolically reflects the mirror concept, highlighting the duality between E!ght's social self and inner self, which is a key theme of the album.

    Additionally, I designed the center labels for sides A and B of the vinyl records, ensuring aesthetic and conceptual consistency with the overall design. My goal was to capture the artist's unique universe while offering a tactile and interactive experience for the audience.
