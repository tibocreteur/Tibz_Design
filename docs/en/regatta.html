"The sails, inflated by the wind, carry not only the sailors but also the dreams of those who gaze at the horizon."

Designed for 12 MED EVENT, this poster for the Toulon Regatta 2025 won the official competition and has been displayed throughout the city of Toulon and its surroundings. The task was to create a new poster while building upon the design of the previous year's poster, bringing a fresh and modern perspective.

I chose to incorporate Pantone's official color for 2025 to reflect a contemporary identity in line with the year of the event. It captures both the energy and elegance of this iconic event. The design highlights the dynamism of the race through moving sailboats, while celebrating Toulon's unique identity with emblematic elements like the Mont Faron cable car.

The minimalist composition, combined with a maritime color palette, reflects the prestige of this competitionand enhances its appeal to the public.
